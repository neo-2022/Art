## Purpose
Defines navigation, links, and anchor conventions for the Art documentation portal.

## Rules
1. Canonical requirements remain in `docs/source/*`.
2. Portal pages are navigation/explanation overlays, not normative contract replacements.
3. Every page must contain a `Source of truth` block.
4. RU and EN pages must keep path parity where required by CI.
